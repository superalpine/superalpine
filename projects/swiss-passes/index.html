Swiss passes

A handbook of Switzerlands' mountain passes



A comprehensive guide to the major road passes in the Swiss Alps with a selection of recommended tours, places to visit and stopping points. The book contains maps, elevation profiles and data points for the known and not so known alpine passes, plotting their history to todays road.
