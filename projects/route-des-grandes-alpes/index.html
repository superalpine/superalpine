Route des Grandes Alpes

The Route des Grandes Alpes or Great Alpine Road is a 700km long itinerary through the French Alps. Starting on the south shore of Lac Leman (Lake Geneva) at the northern tip of the Alps, it traverses some of the highest and most beautiful mountain passes in Europe - ending at the French Riviera on the coastline of the Mediterranean sea.
