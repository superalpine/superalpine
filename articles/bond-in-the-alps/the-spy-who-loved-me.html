Bond in the Alps

The Spy Who Loved Me

Book 1962 n/a
Film 1977 St Moritz Switzerland

Book

The book bares no resemblance to the film.

Film

The opening scene of the Roger Moore film The Spy Who Loved Me culminates with Bond skiing off a cliff and then opening his a parachute which is the flag of the United Kingdom. While the jump scene was shot in Canada, the rest of the ski scenes were filmed high above St. Moritz in Switzerland, which is odd as in the film the whole thing is supposed to take place in Austria.
