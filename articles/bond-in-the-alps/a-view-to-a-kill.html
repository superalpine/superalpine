A view to a Kill

Book 1959 n/a
Film 2006 Switzerland/Italy

Book

The short story From a view to a kill didn't feature the Alps nor did it resemble the film that took (part) of its name

Film

The last of the Roger Moore films, A view to a kill had a pre-credits scene that was supposed to take place in Siberia but most of the scenes were filmed in Iceland. From there a ski chase that follows was shot on the Swiss/Italian border on the Lower Scerscen glacier.
