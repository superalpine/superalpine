Switzerland

Super Alpine magazine Issue 04

In Issue 04 of the Super Alpine magazine we visit Switzerland. We head to the highest part of the Confederation - Valais and cherry picked some of the less travelled aspects of this fascinating country, straying into Vaud, Uri and Fribourg.

Featuring: Crans Montana, Simplon Pass, Lac de Moiry, Zermatt, Andermatt, Susten pass, Grimsel pass, Furka pass, Goldfinger, H.R Giger, Glacier 3000 Ascona, Le Brésilien, Montreux and Grimentz.

  • 195 × 260 mm (7.6 by 10.2 inches)
  • 300gsm soft touch laminated cover
  • 120gsm uncoated inside pages
  • Printed and bound in the United Kingdom on sustainably sourced paper and vegetable-based inks
  • No adverts
