The Super Alpine Magazine

The Super Alpine magazine is a journal of photographs and stories from high altitude. Over 75 photographs in colour and black and white. Featuring three sections, speed, mountains and the high life.

  • The publication is 124 pages and perfect bound.
  • 26 cm x 19.5 cm / 400g
  • The cover is FSC-approved 300gsm uncoated paper, laminated and spot vanished.
  • The inside is printed in full colour on FSC-approved 130gsm uncoated paper.
  • Printed in the United Kingdom.
