Col de Joux Plane

The forgotten climb

The Col de Joux Plane is an often forgotten mountain pass that links the villages of Samoëns and Morzine. Known as one of the tougher climbs on the Tour de France, long and sinuous with a summit of 1700m, it has been featured in no fewer than 12 tours.
