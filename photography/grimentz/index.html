Grimentz

One of the remaining traditional Swiss Villages

Near the end of Val d'Anniviers, Grimentz has been called one of Europe's most beautiful villages. The narrow, pedestrianised streets are lined with sun-blackened wooden houses adorned with hundreds of red geraniums. Standing out amongst these old chalets and building is the ultra-modern ski lift station.
