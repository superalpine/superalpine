Cirque du Fer à Cheval

At one end of the Giffre valley in the French Alps lies a horseshoe of mountains.

Cirque du Fer à Cheval is a horseshoe of limestone cliffs forming a natural amphitheatre that allows for nearly 360-degree views. The second-largest amphitheatre horseshoe in France, this nature reserve has over 30 waterfalls descending its 700m escarpments and is looked down upon by peaks rising to 3000m.
