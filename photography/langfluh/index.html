Längfluh

A rocky outcrop of the Pennine Alps dividing the Fee Glacier

The Längfluh is a rocky outcrop of the Pennine Alps, dividing the Fee Glacier above Saas Fee in the canton of Valais, Switzerland.

The Fee Glacier lies east of the Mischabel range, between the summit of Dom on the north and Allalinhorn on the south above the village.
