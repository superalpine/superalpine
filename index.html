Freeskiing in Avoriaz

The unique architecture of Avoriaz in the Portes du Soleil area of France was designed in the '60s by Jacques Labro, Jean-Jacques Orzoni and Jean-Marc Roques. These buildings designed to echo the surrounding terrain are the playground for freeskier Richard Permin in a short film.
