Jaun Pass

Fribourg / Bern

The Jaun Pass or Col de Bellegarde sits at 1509 metres in the canton of Bern and was opened in 1878. The road winds its way up the Nieder Simmental valley and crosses the language divide from French to German.

Location
46°35’27.0”N, 7°20’20.0”E
