Swiss Passes

The Julier pass connects Silvaplana in the Engadin valley with Tiefencastel. The pass road was constructed in the 1820s to replace the old Septimer pass.

Location
46°28’19.2"N, 9°43’44.4”E
SILVAPLANA > TIEFENCASTEL
Distance (km) 42
Altitude (m) 2284
Start Altitude (m) 1815
End Altitude (m) 884
