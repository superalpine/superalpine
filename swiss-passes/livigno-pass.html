Livigno Pass

The Livigno Pass or Forcola di Livigno runs from Livigno in Italy to the border with Switzerland at La Motta. The road to Livigno starts in the Swiss canton of Graubünden at La Drossa, crossing the border into Italy before Livigno and back into Switzerland before the customs post at La Motta and onto the Bernina pass.
