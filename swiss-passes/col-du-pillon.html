Vaud

Col du Pillon

Col du Pillon is one of the least exciting drives over the Swiss Passes but makes up for it with a dramatic zenith. Making up part of the route from Aigle in canton Vaud to Gstaad in canton Berne the Col du presents an often spectacular landscape as the route is overlooked by the glaciers and mountains like the strikingly angular Sommet des Diablerets.



Summit coordinates 46°21’25.0”N, 7°12’56.0"E
Summit Altitude 1546 metres
Distance 29 Kilometres
Start Le Sepey 950 metres
End Altitude (m) Gstaad 1050 metres
