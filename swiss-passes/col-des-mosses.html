Swiss Passes

From vineyards to gorges, Col des Mosses is a busy but beautiful pass that starts at Le Sepey and ends at Château-d'Œx. One of the less remarkable summits, the sign is barely noticed in a large parking area used for skiers in the winter. In the summer the area is mostly deserted.

Location
46°23’58.7”N, 7°06’07.9”E
Le Sepey > Château-d'Œx
Distance (km) 22
Altitude (m) 1445
Start Altitude (m) 950
End Altitude (m) 958
