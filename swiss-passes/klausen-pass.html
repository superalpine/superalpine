Klausen pass

Uri/Glarus

The Klausen pass sits at 1948 metres at its highest point and connects Altdorf in the canton of Uri with Linthal in the canton of Glarus. The road is normally closed between October and May, due to the snow.

Statistics

Summit Location 46°52’10.3”N, 8°51’20.9”E
Summit Altitude 1948 metres
Distance 47 Kilometres
Start Altdorf 432 metres
End Linthal 650 metres
Opens June to mid-October
