Umbrail Pass

The Umbrail Pass sits on the far side of Switzerland, the highest paved road in the country - it starts in Val Mustair and ends at the Italian border.

Location
46°32’35.0”N, 10°26’02.0”E
Val Mustair > Italian border
Distance (km) 17
Altitude (m) 2503
Start Altitude (m) 1375
End Altitude (m) 2502
