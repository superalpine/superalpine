Bernina Pass

Linking the high life of Switzerland with the Italian lakes

The Bernina Pass takes its name from the Bernina mountain range and connects Pontresina with Poschiavo. The pass is well known for it scenic Bernina Express train and the annual Bernina Gran Turismo event - a competitive mountain Hillclimb for 80 selected classic and vintage cars that has run each fall for the last seven years.



Summit coordinates 46°24’41.1”N, 10°01’36.1”E
Summit Altitude 2330 metres
Distance 33 kilometres
Start Pontresina 1805 metres
End Poschiavo 1014 metres
Opens All year

* All year donates the pass is cleared of snow but maybe closed on some days
