Uri/Ticino

Gotthard Pass

The Saint Gotthard pass traverses the Saint-Gotthard Massif and connects northern and southern Switzerland. The pass runs from Hospental in the German-speaking canton of Uri and Airolo in the Italian-speaking canton of Ticino. The old Tremolo road runs parralel to the pass and a tunnel bypasses both.



Summit coordinates 46°33’22.5”N, 8°34’04.0”E
Summit Altitude 2106 metres
Distance 23 kilometres
Start Hospental 1493 metres
End Airolo 1175 metres
Opens June to mid-October
