Gotthard Pass

Uri / Ticino

Saint Gotthard spoiled the alps for choice with multiple ways of traversing the Gotthard massif. The Saint Gotthard pass traverses the Saint-Gotthard Massif and connects northern and southern Switzerland. The pass runs from Andermatt in the German-speaking canton of Uri and Airolo in the Italian-speaking canton of Ticino.

Location
46°33’22.5”N, 8°34’04.0”E
HOSPENTAL > Airolo
Distance (km) 23
Altitude (m) 2106
