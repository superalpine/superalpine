Ofen pass

The Ofen or Fuorn pass is 2149 metres at it's highest point. The name is based on the ovens that were used in ironworks in the area. The ruins of these ovens can still be seen from nearby trails. The pass connects Zernez in the Engadin valley with Val Müstair, crossing the Swiss National Park in Switzerland.
