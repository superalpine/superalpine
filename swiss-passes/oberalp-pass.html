Oberalp Pass

Graubünden / Uri

The Oberalp Pass sits at 2044 meters connecting the cantons of Graubünden and Uri between Disentis/Mustér and Andermatt. The railway follows the course of the pass with a station at the summit.

Location
46°39'54.0"N, 8°40'26.0"E
