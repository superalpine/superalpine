Lukmanier Pass

Graubünden/Ticino

The Lukmanier pass runs from Disentis in Graubünden through to the Blenio valley and Biasca in the canton of Ticino. It is generally open in the winter closing only overnight.

Summit Location 46°33’57.7”N, 8°48’05.2”E
Summit Altitude 1915 metres
Distance 59 Kilometres
Start Disentis 1130 metres
End Biasca 301 metres
Opens June to mid-October
